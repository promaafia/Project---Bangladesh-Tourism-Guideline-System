Aswini Kumar Town Hall is situated in the middle of barisal town. It was once the only cultural centre of southern Bengal. From the beginning it was used as a venue for meetings, plays, fairs etc. As it is situated in the centre of the town, the people of Barisal know it better as Town Hall. It was named in memory of aswini kumar datta, who had dedicated his life to the development of Barisal.

Hall of Barisal district! In 1906, Advertisers attacks King bahadur's habeli rally carried out by the police. Aswini Kumar Dutta proposed to create remembrance of the event . He Aswini Kumar datta is the president and saratcandra guha secretary of the committee was formed to create the hall in 1920. In 15 October of the same year, purchased the land from lawyers kanta bandyopadhyaya and the king bahadurer. But according to a foundation set up in the Hall in the next October. During the creation of 7 November 1923 Aswini Kumar Dutta died. They are held his remember in this hall. Aswini Kumar Town Hall is the name adopted by the remember. 1930 construction of Hall is fully completed. In 24 February of that year Trust deed is done. Construction and land is about 40 thousand money is spent. The construction ended by surplus funds of Non-cooperation movement and khelaphat Andolan conference. The hall was created directly by the people who have worked Durga Mohan Sen, Engineer Jotish Chandra lahiri , Wahed Raja Chowdhury, Hashem Ali Khan, Badsha Mia, Ismail Khan. Their contribution is unforgettable.
